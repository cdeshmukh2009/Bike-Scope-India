Bike Purchase Guide

Use this quick framework before finalizing your bike.

1. Define Your Use Case

Daily commute, weekend touring, performance riding, or mixed use.

2. Set Total Budget

Include ex-showroom price, insurance, accessories, fuel/charging, and maintenance.

3. Check Comfort and Ergonomics

Seat height, riding posture, and weight balance should match your physique.

4. Compare Features and Safety

ABS, traction control, lighting quality, tyres, and braking confidence.

5. Test Ride and Service Network

Always test ride and verify nearby service support before paying.
