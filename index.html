Brief History of Indian Bikes

India's bike journey began with durable classic motorcycles in the mid-20th century, then expanded rapidly in the 1980s and 1990s with fuel-efficient commuter models. In the 2000s onward, the market evolved into a mix of sport, cruiser, adventure, and now electric bikes, making India one of the world's most dynamic two-wheeler landscapes.
